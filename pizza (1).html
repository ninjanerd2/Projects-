This is the Pizza Recipe

Assemble and Baking

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup (250g) pizza sauce (or crushed San Marzano tomatoes with a pinch of salt)
    • 4 cups (454g) shredded or torn fresh mozzarella cheese
    • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, basil, mushrooms, etc.)
    • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Crank your oven as high as it will go (ideally 240°C to 285°C / 475°F to 550°F). If using a pizza stone, let it preheat in the oven for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Stretch the Dough: Using your fingers, press and stretch the dough into your desired circle thickness, leaving a slightly thicker edge for the crust. Transfer to a lightly floured baking sheet or a pizza peel dusted with semolina flour.
  3. Top the Pizza: Brush the crust lightly with olive oil. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce (don't over-sauce, or the crust will get soggy) and top with mozzarella and your favorite ingredients.
  4. Bake: Bake for 10 to 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbling, golden brown, and the crust is crisp.
